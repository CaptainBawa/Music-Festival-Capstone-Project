"Hello! Music Lovers"

GLOBAL MUSIC FESTIVAL CELEBRATING CULTURAL UNITY OF HUMANITY THROUGH MUSIC

The festival aims to promote the idea that despite cultural differences, music can be a unifying force that brings people together. The program may involve performances by musicians from different parts of the world, workshops, and cultural exhibitions. Overall, the festival is a celebration of the richness of human culture and the power of music to connect people from different backgrounds.

2023.12.24(FRI) ~ 27(SUN)

@ Ghana, Dunkwa-On-Offin Mfuom Stadium

Main Program

Music Production

Showcase diverse music genres from around the world, like African, Indian & American music, etc.

Music Workshops

Organize global music workshops to teach about diverse traditions, instruments, and styles

Cultural Exhibitions

Display cultural exhibitions featuring art, history, and traditions of diverse cultures.

Discussions and Talks

Organize talks exploring music's role in cultural exchange, social justice, and community.

Children's Program

Kids' program to introduce diverse music traditions and styles from around the world.
